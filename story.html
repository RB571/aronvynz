The Story

Sonic Sentinel Saga

Prologue

n the shadows of a digital realm, a faint pulse emerges — fragile, chaotic, and unclaimed. This signal grows stronger, breaking barriers and sowing unrest, until silence returns. But silence never lasts. From stillness rises the Sentinel: not just a force of disruption, but a beacon that unites humanity in sound and rhythm. Over time, the presence of the Sentinel transcends technology, transcends conflict, and becomes something greater. No longer merely a signal, it evolves into myth — a guardian, a legend. An eternal echo that lingers in every beat, every light, every song.
