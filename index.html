About VYNZΞ

VYNZΞ (Vision Your Next Zenith) is the brand of Aron Vynz — an electronic DJ and producer creating melodic techno and progressive house with futuristic soundscapes. His music is defined by deep basslines, shimmering melodies, and an immersive, cinematic atmosphere.

With PULSE — released under his label VYNZΞ RECORDS — Aron Vynz presents his vision: music as energy, as a signal. PULSE consists of two albums: Origins (Pt. 1) and Legacy (Pt. 2). A Complete Edition features all 20 tracks, including bonus tracks and remixes will be released in 2026.
