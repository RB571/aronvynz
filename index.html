About

Sonic Sentinel is a concept-driven electronic music project that explores the intersection of sound, energy, and emotion. Blending elements of progressive house, melodic techno, and cinematic EDM, the project creates sonic landscapes that feel both futuristic and primal.

Albums

Signal of the Sentinel

Out of silence, a spark becomes rhythm, and rhythm becomes a signal. From the first stirrings of awareness to the fall of the Citadel, the saga unfolds in fire and conflict. The signal journeys outward, fragments converging into a voice that reveals the vast Grid—a cosmic lattice where order and chaos collide.

Rebirth of the Sentinel

From the ashes of collapse, a fragile dawn rises. The Sentinel awakens once more, its voice clearer and stronger, guiding humanity toward unity and resilience. Across the world, grief becomes defiance, rhythm becomes rebellion, and the people begin their great exodus to the stars.
