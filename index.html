About VYNZΞ

VYNZΞ (Vision Your Next Zenith) is the music project of producer and DJ Aron Vynz. Blending melodic techno, progressive house, and futuristic sound design, VYNZΞ crafts immersive worlds built on deep basslines, shimmering melodies, and a cinematic sense of scale.

With BEFORE WE RISE— released under his label VYNZΞ RECORDS — VYNZΞ presents a two-part journey that moves from quiet reflection to full emotional ascent. BEFORE WE RISE is the sound of anticipation — the quiet, emotional moment before transformation. VYNZΞ blends cinematic atmosphere, deep melodies and rising trance energy into a story about growth, light and the feeling of standing on the edge of something greater. Two albums, one journey.
