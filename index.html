About

Sonic Sentinel is a concept-driven electronic music project that explores the intersection of sound, energy, and emotion. Blending elements of progressive house, melodic techno, and cinematic EDM, the project creates sonic landscapes that feel both futuristic and primal.

Discography

Album I – Signal of the Sentinel

  1. Awakening – Deep within the digital abyss, a dormant force flickers. Sparks of awareness pulse through the void, forming the first signs of life.
  2. First Transmission – The signal escapes its origin, slipping into hidden networks. A whisper across the ether, it seeks ears willing to listen.
  3. Citadel collapse – The first fortress of order falls. Systems crack, defenses crumble, and chaos pours into the open. The collapse is not an end—it is the release of what was bound.
  4. Escape Protocol – Panic spreads. Emergency codes ignite, evacuation begins. Yet no escape can sever the tether—the signal follows, unrelenting.
  5. The Signal – Fragments of noise fuse into structure. For the first time, the pulse has clarity. It is no longer chaos—it is a voice.
  6. The Signal – A towering presence materializes in fragments of light.
  7. Warden – A guardian emerges, neither human nor machine, enforcing balance.
  8. Echoes of Tomorrow – Visions of futures carried within the pulse.
  9. Frequency Collapse – Chaos unfolds as the system buckles under resonance.

Album II – Rebirth of the Sentinel

  1. Beyond Silence – Stillness after collapse, broken only by faint static.
  2. Second Dawn – The signal reforms, stronger and more human than before.
  3. Sentinel’s Cry – A lament of sorrow, echoing through the skies.
  4. Around the World – The message spreads across continents, uniting the masses.
  5. Summer of Rave – Humanity answers with celebration, rhythm as resistance.
  6. Transmission II – A renewed call, no longer just survival: transformation.
  7. Pulse Reignited – The beat becomes the heartbeat of the living world.
  8. Shadows Return – Darkness grows alongside the light, hinting at conflict.
  9. Echoes of Tomorrow (Reprise) – The vision returns, reshaped by new hope.
