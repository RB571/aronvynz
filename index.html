ARON VYNZ — PULSE

VYNZΞ (Vision Your Next Zenith) is the brand of Aron Vynz — an electronic DJ and producer creating melodic techno and progressive house with futuristic soundscapes. His music is defined by deep basslines, shimmering melodies, and an immersive, cinematic atmosphere.

With PULSE — released under his label VYNZΞ RECORDS — Aron Vynz presents his vision: music as energy, as a signal. PULSE consists of two main albums — Origins (Pt. 1) , Rebirth (Pt. 2) and Legacy (Pt. 3). An Anthology Edition will follow, featuring all 21 tracks alongside additional remixes and extended versions.
